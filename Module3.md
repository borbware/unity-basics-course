## Module project 3: Dungeon crawler
<!-- _backgroundColor: #257179 -->

⭐ MVP
  * Top-down movement
  * Tile-based level (bigger than one screen!)
  * Animated player character
    * Attack (punch or shoot)
  * Enemies that hurt the player
  * Health item pickups
